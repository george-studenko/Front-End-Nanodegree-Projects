Fischer's lovebird

While most Fischer's lovebirds are green, several color variations have been bred. The blue variation is predominant.

  • Scientific name: Agapornis fischeri
  • Higher classification: Lovebird
  • Rank: Species

The Fischer's lovebird is a small parrot species of the Agapornis genus. They were originally discovered in the late 19th century, and were first bred in the United States in 1926.
